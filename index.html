Suniaga Organization is a non-profit organization that collaborates closely with other non-profit organizations to assist immigrants globally who are facing humanitarian crises.

Our mission is to contribute to the pursuit of peace and provide legal pathways for individuals from countries such as El Salvador, Haiti, Honduras, Nepal, Nicaragua, Somalia, South Sudan, Sudan, Syria, Venezuela, and Yemen to immigrate to Canada, France, Germany, Mexico, Panama, Spain, the United Kingdom, the United States, and other countries around the world.

Each applicant undergoes a rigorous selection process, fully sponsored by our non-profit partners, ensuring that no financial burden falls on the applicant. Since our inception in 2016 by the Suniaga Family, our organization has successfully facilitated the safe relocation of over 85 individuals, providing them with opportunities for legal immigration to various countries.

As our organization continues to grow, we are thrilled to announce our expansion efforts, enabling us to assist even more applicants in the future. Suniaga Organization remains unwavering in our commitment to supporting immigrants in need, offering hope and opportunity to those seeking refuge and a better life.
